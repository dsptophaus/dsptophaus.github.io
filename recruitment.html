Recruitment | Why Rush?

Rush: a sequence of social events sponsored by a fraternity or sorority for prospective members prior to bidding and pledging, also known as recruitment.

Why should you Rush with DSP?

"At Delta Sig, we believe in Building Better Men. We encourage our members to strive to be the best they can be in academics as well as in their social life. By joining Delta Sig, you are entering into the unbreakable bond of brotherhood with the finest men on campus, a bond secured by the shared values of culture, harmony, and friendship. Values emboldened by an especially diverse group of brothers. While many fraternities boast similar values, what separates us from the flock is that we have so much fun doing it."
- James Tracy (Recruitment Director)
